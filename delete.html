Delete Your Account & Data

Kailasa · Shiva Manifestation App — Last updated: 22 July 2026

Option 1 — Delete all data from within the app

You can erase all your data directly inside Kailasa without contacting us:

  1. Open Kailasa on your device.
  2. Tap the More tab.
  3. Tap Reset all data.
  4. Confirm the action when prompted.

This permanently removes all your local data and, if you are signed in, also clears your cloud profile and all associated records from our servers.

Option 2 — Request full account deletion via email

If you prefer, or if you are unable to access the app, you can request complete account deletion by emailing us:

kailasaapp@gmail.com

Include the email address you used to create your Kailasa account. We will process your request and delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days, and confirm by reply once complete.

What gets deleted

Permanently deleted

  • Email address and hashed password
  • Profile data (name, chosen manifestation goal, mantra preference)
  • Course progress, streak count, and badge data
  • Japa mala round counts and pooja records
  • Sankalpa Journal entries
  • Pro/Sadhu tier status
  • All locally stored data (localStorage)

What we do not retain

We do not retain any of your personal data after deletion. There is no retention period. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Third-party data

If you purchased Kailasa Pro through the Google Play Store, your purchase record is managed by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We cannot delete Google's transaction records on your behalf.

Questions?

For any concerns about your data, contact us at kailasaapp@gmail.com
